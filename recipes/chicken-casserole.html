Chicken Casserole

Baked chicken dish with cheese, vegetables, and a crust on top.

  • 1lb fresh chicken breast
  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • Bread crumbs
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Green beans

Steps

  1. Gather above ingredients
  2. Cut chicken breasts
  3. Fill casserole dish with chicken and cream of chicken
  4. Add layer of cheese on top
  5. Add layer of breadcrumbs on top of cheese
  6. Bake at 350* for 35 minutes

Fried Bolgna Sandwich

Fried Bolgna Sandwich with Cheese.

  • 1 piece sliced bolgna
  • 2 pieces white bread
  • 1 piece cheddar cheese
  • Mayonnaise (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather above ingredients
  2. Fry piece of bolgna on skillet at medium temperature
  3. Place cheese on top of bolgna when it's done to your liking
  4. Put bolgna with cheese on bread
  5. Add mayonnaise

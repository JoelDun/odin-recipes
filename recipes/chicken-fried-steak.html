Chicken Fried Steak with Gravy

  • 3 pounds cube steak
  • 1 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all purpose flower
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingriedients together
  2. Mix all wet ingredients together
  3. Dip each piece of meat in the wet bowl followed by the dry bowl
  4. Heat the oil in a large skillet
  5. Fry all of the meat
  6. Add in the flower to the leftover vegetable oil
  7. Whisk in milk until desired consistency
  8. Serve the gravy along side of the meat
